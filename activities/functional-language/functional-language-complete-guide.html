Welcome! Let's Learn Functional Language Together

Functional language helps you communicate effectively in real-life situations. This guide covers 12 essential categories of functional language that you'll use every day in English conversations.

What is Functional Language? Functional language includes phrases and expressions we use to accomplish specific communication goals - like making suggestions, giving advice, apologizing, or expressing opinions. These are the "tools" you need for successful communication!

Each section includes common phrases, examples, and practice exercises. Work through each category at your own pace!
