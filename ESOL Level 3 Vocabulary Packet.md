**Transition** \[tran-zish-uhn\] \- The process of change from one situation to another

**U**

**Under pressure** \[uhn-der presh-er\] \- In a stressful situation

**Urgently** \[ur-juhnt-lee\] \- Very quickly or needing fast help

**Utilities** \[yoo-til-i-teez\] \- Basic services in a home, like electricity, water, and gas

**V**

**Vacant** \[vey-kuhnt\] \- Empty or available for rent

**Vocational** \[voh-key-shuhn-uhl\] \- Education, training, or skills that prepare a person for a specific job or trade

**W**

**Waived** \[weyvd\] \- Not required to pay

**Warranty** \[wawr-uhn-tee\] \- A written promise to fix or replace a product

**Well-being** \[wel bee-ing\] \- Overall health and happiness
