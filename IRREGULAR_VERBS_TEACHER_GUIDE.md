## ğŸ’¡ Teaching Tips

### Before the Game
1. **Brief Introduction**: "This game teaches verb patterns, not memorization"
2. **Show Examples**: Demonstrate one group (Group 1: cost, cut, hit - all stay the same)
3. **Set Expectations**: "You need 80% to unlock the next group"
4. **Reassure Them**: "You can retry as many times as needed"

### While Students Play
1. **Let them struggle** (it's productive struggle!)
2. **Observe which groups** they struggle with most
3. **Provide hints**: Point to the pattern hint on the screen
4. **Encourage retries**: "You got 75%, try again!"

### After Completing All 10 Groups
1. **Celebrate** - They've earned this!
2. **Review** - What patterns were easiest/hardest?
3. **Apply** - Have them find these verbs in real texts
4. **Extend** - Discuss other irregular verbs not in the game
