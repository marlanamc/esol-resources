Reading

Health records are stored securely in electronic systems. Your medical information is recorded during each visit and is protected by privacy laws. Test results are sent to your MyChart account automatically, so you can access them online.

Appointments are scheduled through the office or online portal. Messages can be sent to your doctor through MyChart, and responses are usually received within a few days. Your personal information is protected at all times by security measures.

Passive voice is useful when describing health records because it focuses on what happens to the information, not who handles it. For example, "Records are stored" focuses on the records, not the staff. "Information is protected" tells us what happens to the information, not who protects it.

1) How are health records stored and protected?
2) How can you access your test results and communicate with your doctor?
