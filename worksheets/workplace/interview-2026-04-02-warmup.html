Level up: End with "Do you have any questions for me?" and prepare 1-2 questions to ask.

Reading

Job interviews can be stressful, but preparation helps. Practice your answers before the interview. Be ready to talk about your experience, skills, and why you want the job. Think of examples from your past work.

For the "weakness" question, choose something real but not serious. Explain how you're working to improve. Always be honest but positive.

1) What should you do before an interview?
2) How should you answer the "weakness" question?
3) What is one strength you would tell an interviewer?

Writing Homework

6–8 sentences
Prompt: Imagine you are interviewing for a retail cashier position at a grocery store. Write your answer to "Tell me about yourself" for this job. Include your experience, skills, and goals.

Optional challenge: Record yourself saying this answer. Listen and improve.
