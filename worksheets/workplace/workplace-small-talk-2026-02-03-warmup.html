Reading

Small talk is casual, friendly conversation at work. It helps you build a connection with colleagues. Safe topics include the weather, time off, and your schedule. Avoid politics, religion, or very personal topics.

Use simple questions: "How was your time off?" "What are you working on?" "Do you have plans for tonight?" Listen and ask one follow-up question. Then you can say "Nice talking to you!" or "I should get back to work."
