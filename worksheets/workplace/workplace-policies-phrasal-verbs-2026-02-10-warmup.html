Reading

Every workplace has policies and procedures. Phrasal verbs are common in workplace instructions. "Clock in" means record when you arrive. "Clock out" means record when you leave. "Fill out this form" means complete it, and "turn it in" means submit it.

If you don't understand a policy, ask your supervisor to go over it with you. If you have questions later, follow up with HR. You may also need to log in to a computer to read or sign new policies.

1) What does "fill out" mean?
2) When should you call out?
3) What should you do if you don't understand a policy?
