Reading

Every workplace has policies and procedures. Phrasal verbs are common in workplace instructions. "Fill out this form" means complete it. "Turn it in" means submit it. "Sign in" means record when you arrive.

If you don't understand a policy, ask your supervisor to go over it with you. If you have questions later, follow up with HR.

1) What does "fill out" mean?
2) When should you call in?
3) What should you do if you don't understand a policy?
