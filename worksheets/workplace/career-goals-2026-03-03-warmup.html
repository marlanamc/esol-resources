Reading

Long-term goals help you plan your future. Future perfect shows actions that will be completed before a future point. "By 2030, I will have worked at this company for ten years."

This tense emphasizes completed actions in the future. It helps you think about what you will have achieved by certain dates.

1) By the end of this year, what will you have completed?
2) What is the difference between "I will work" and "I will have worked"?
3) What will you have achieved by next year?
