-ed Endings Warmup Review

Arrival warmup: students join and answer with letters in chat.

Regular past participles in result form often end in -ed, but pronunciation changes.

/t/

After voiceless sounds (p, k, f, s, sh, ch, x): walked, washed, watched, asked

/d/

After voiced sounds (vowels, b, g, v, z, m, n, l, r): played, cleaned, lived, opened

/id/

After /t/ or /d/ endings: wanted, needed, started, visited
