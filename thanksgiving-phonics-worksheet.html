2. Reading: "Thankful for Family"

Read the story and notice the TH, F, B, and V sounds:

This week is Thanksgiving. Ana and her family get ready for a small dinner with friends.

The father cooks fish and vegetables. The mother makes a thick pumpkin soup. Ana helps by cutting fruit for a fresh fall salad.

They talk about what they are thankful for this year. Ana is thankful for her friends. Her mother is thankful for good weather. Her father is thankful for fun moments with his family.

They smile, eat, and say thank you for a warm evening together.

5. Pronunciation Practice Sentences

Read these sentences aloud. Focus on the TH, F, B, and V sounds.

1. I am thankful for my family.
2. The father cooks a thick fish.
3. This food is very fresh.
4. Ana can think of five things she loves.
5. The fall weather is warm this week.

6. Bonus: Fill in the Blanks

Complete each sentence with a word that has TH, F, B, or V sound.

1. I am _______________ for my friends. (starts with TH)
2. My _______________ makes delicious pumpkin pie. (starts with F)
3. We eat _______________ at Thanksgiving dinner. (starts with F)
4. _______________ is a special holiday in November. (starts with TH)
5. I _______________ my family is healthy. (starts with TH)
